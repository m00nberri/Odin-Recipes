Delicious Udon

Udon is a delicious dish of noodles and hearty broth. It's great to eat on cold days, and even hot days if you eat it cold.

This is a recipe that was handed down to me by my great grandparents. They were udon farmers. It always tastes amazing and I hope you can enjoy making it too.

Ingredients

Main

  • Udon
  • Meat
  • Hot Water

Optional

  • Green Onions
  • Agedama

Instructions

  1. Add the Udon to the hot water
  2. When the Udon is tender, add the meat
  3. Add any additional ingredients you want
  4. Enjoy!
