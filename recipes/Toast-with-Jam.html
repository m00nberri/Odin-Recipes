Delicious Toast with Jam

Toast with jam is a very traditional dish dating back to the dark ages. It was a great way for field serfs to start their morning right with a sweet kick of energy.

This is a recipe that was handed down to me by my great grandparents. They were toast farmers. It always tastes amazing and I hope you can enjoy making it too.

Ingredients

Main

  • Bread
  • Jam

Optional

  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Make the bread into toast
  2. When the toast is done, spread the jam on top
  3. Add any additional ingredients you want
  4. Enjoy!
