Delicious Macaroni Cheese

Macaroni Cheese is a delicious dish of noodles and hearty cheese. It's great to eat on cold days, and you can add so many different ingredients.

This is a recipe that was handed down to me by my great grandparents. They were macaroni cheese farmers. It always tastes amazing and I hope you can enjoy making it too.

Ingredients

Main

  • Macaroni
  • Cheese

Optional

  • Bacon
  • Tomatoes
  • Mustard

Instructions

  1. Cook the macaroni
  2. Melt the cheese
  3. When both are done, mix them together
  4. Place it in the oven and bake for 30 minutes because if you don't bake your macaroni cheese you are dead to me
  5. Add any additional ingredients you want
  6. Enjoy!
